Open the app, enter when your baby woke up, and get a full day schedule — instantly.

## Privacy Policy

ninna is built with your privacy in mind.

- **No data collection.** ninna does not collect, store, or share your personal information.
- **Everything stays on your device.** All information you enter — wake times, nap history, preferences — is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.
- **No tracking.** The app does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising of any kind.
- **No account required.** You never need to create an account or provide an email address to use ninna.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does ninna calculate the schedule?**  
ninna uses your baby's age to determine appropriate wake windows, then builds a realistic nap schedule forward from the morning wake time you enter.

**Does ninna work offline?**  
Yes, completely. ninna works 100% offline with no internet connection required.

**How do I reset my data?**  
You can delete and reinstall the app to start fresh, or use the reset option in the app's settings.

**What age range does ninna support?**  
ninna is designed for babies and toddlers from newborn through approximately 3 years old.
